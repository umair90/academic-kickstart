Description

Feed Your Bird is a FREE - fun-filled logical puzzle game. The objective is to release a bird from a cage in order to save it from starving. Different objects are dropped from different angles to collapse the cage and release the bird. The game is simple yet interesting as it involves logical solutions by invoking rules of physics and your sixth sense.

Some Key Features of the Game:

^ High quality Graphics

^ 60 Completely Different puzzles to solve

^ Puzzle Hints Playback if you are stuck during the game

^ Lives Scenario with UNLIMITED Bird Lives at each level to make your Puzzle Game play worthwhile [NEW!]


In this challenging puzzle game, you can master a level by applying different methods. The difficulty of level increases after every stage whereas each stage has four different levels which will engage you in a specific logical mode.


Beautifully designed themes are more than just impressive, ensuring a fascinating and worthwhile playtime for you along with a captivating music to complement the brain's activity.


The game is designed both for kids and adults, with adults having a chance to remove any rust in the brain and children having the opportunity to polish their skills.


The game introduces AUTOMATED HINTS via visual aids, just in case you get stuck somewhere during the game. The game also includes a complete understanding of how any level can be completed after pin-pointing an area of focus. These are properly revealed with their explanations in the end, to keep the fun intact. PAID lives are available if you run low of Bird lives; with the total 60 Levels things get more interesting.
